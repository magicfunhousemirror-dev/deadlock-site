• How To Play

Lady Jeanne Geist is a ruthless offensive character, using her own lifeforce to deal more damage to her foes and taking it back using lifesteal mechanics. Her signature feature, The green clawed arm replacing her right arm, is called Oathbreaker and allows her to keep her youth and spirit powers despite being over 100 years old by stealing the souls of others. But Miss Geist doesn't plan on keeping him for long
Lady Geist's Repertoire
Essence Bomb
Lady Geist sacrifices a portion of her own health to lob an explosive bomb at enemies. It arms briefly after landing before detonating, dealing heavy area damage. The bomb can stick to walls and ceilings, exploding within its radius — meaning enemies behind cover aren't always safe if the blast circle wraps around them. At higher upgrade levels, it leaves a toxic pool on the ground that continues to deal damage over time. A core poke and wave-clear tool, but it costs you HP to throw, so every miss hurts twice.

Life Drain
Lady Geist creates a tether to an enemy that drains their health over time and heals her in return. The target must remain in line of sight and within range for the drain to keep working. Insider Gaming You can shoot and use other abilities while draining, but your movement speed is cut in half. At higher levels, the drain also silences the target, making it devastating against ability-dependent heroes. This is her primary sustain tool and what makes her so oppressive in extended 1v1s.

Malice
Lady Geist sacrifices some of her health to launch blood shards that apply a stack of Malice on hit. Each stack slows the victim and increases the damage they take from her, with the slow diminishing over time. Stack it multiple times to shred someone's movement and turn them into a glass cannon for your follow-up burst. It also serves a sneaky secondary purpose — a quick way to deliberately lower her own HP before using Soul Exchange.

Soul Exchange
For her UltimateLady Geist swaps her current health value with an enemy target's. If she's been whittled down to 10% health and her enemy is sitting comfortably at 80%, one button flip reverses the entire fight. It's her signature ability and makes her a massive 1v1 threat, capable of turning an otherwise losing situation entirely in her favor. At higher levels it also grants her a burst of Spirit Power, Fire Rate, and Spirit Resistance on cast, turning the swap into an immediate offensive window.
