Welcome to Deadlock

New York, 1930s — but not the one you know. The supernatural didn't fade into legend here. It moved in. And now two ancient gods are at war, each commanding six mortals to fight, die, and fight again across the twisted streets of a city that never should have existed.

Deadlock is Valve's genre-defying 6v6 battleground — part third-person shooter, part MOBA, entirely its own thing. Master a roster of characters with deep ability sets and lethal gunplay. Farm Souls from fallen enemies. Build your loadout on the fly. Outmaneuver, outshoot, and out-think the opposition across a labyrinthine map dripping in atmosphere. Air-dash through gothic alleyways. Slide into cover as your abilities come online. Coordinate with your team to push lanes, shred towers, and ultimately destroy the enemy Patron — an elder god's effigy standing between you and a possible life changing boon from your Patron.
Pick a side. Earn your Souls. Make your god proud.

As in most MOBA-type games, the area where the players battle one another is split into lanes where groups of people fight to destroy various mcguffins as visualized on this map by the red diamond shapes

Players seperate into groups of two and fight off hordes of small enemies while collecting souls, this game's currency. Souls are then used to buy items that power up your character
