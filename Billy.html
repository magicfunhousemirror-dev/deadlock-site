• How To Play

Billy is just a dude with a goat for a head. No story other than that. He's a powerful melee-based combat monster who can pummel enemies if not handled with caution. His abilities look for ways to get up close and the kill the opponent. All this guy wants to do is lay waste and get wasted.
Billy's Kit
Bashdown!
Bashdown is on of Billy's signature abilities. Billy can use this ability to smash the ground below with his bat, blasting an area around the point of impact. this attack counts as a melee attack which is normally a riskier close-up attack with the bat. Few characters have abilities that deal this sort of melee damage. This allows any abilities or upgrades that care about the melee attack have increased power
These Items are especially good for Billy and synergize with Smashdown:
Rising Ram
The Rising Ram ability acts as a way to close the gap betweeen you and your opponents. Allowing you to get in additional close quarters strikes such as a Bashdown or melee atack. As seen in this clip, this skill lifts both Billy and the target into the air and from there you can hit them with another attack
These items work with Rising Ram to capitalize on the better positioning on can attain:
Blasted
The second of billy's signature effects, Blasted gives Billy a boost that incentivizes hitting an opponent with a melee attack by giving you extra health, extra damage, extra ammo, and an increase in the effects length. You can activate this when you are in a major battle so as to keep yourself in the game for longer stretchs of time without even having to reload
Chain Gang
Chain Gang is Billy's final Ultimate ability. It uses large spiked chains to force enemies into close combat and lets you bring weakened enemies back for more. This move is especially useful when enemies are trying to flee as it prevents movement abilities and pulls them towards you, giving you a chance to hit for more melee damage
